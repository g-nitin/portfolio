About

I'm a researcher interested in making language models more reliable, efficient, and grounded. My work sits at the intersection of long-context modeling, retrieval, and evaluation — building systems that reason over large amounts of information without losing the thread.

Currently finishing my M.S. in Computer Science at USC, where I work in the NLP group. Before that I studied at IIT Delhi. Outside research you'll find me running the trails around Los Angeles or restoring a film camera that almost works.
